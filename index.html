About

A Practitioner Who Became a Scholar.
A Scholar Who Remains a Builder.

Dr. He Xinhao — known as Onicek He — is a professor, entrepreneur, and organizational founder whose work spans the full arc from real-world practice to rigorous academic inquiry. Over more than a decade, he has built platforms, designed institutional mechanisms, and advanced foundation development, while simultaneously developing a systematic framework for understanding how individuals, organizations, brands, foundations, and families construct value that lasts.

Unlike those who come from a single discipline or a single professional track, his path crosses multiple systems: the commercial world of brand, creativity, and platform structure; the complex coordination systems of organizations; the institutional logic of foundations and nonprofits; and the translation of experience into communicable, iterable methodology through doctoral training and research.

He is not studying a single management topic. He is working on a larger question: how long-term value systems are truly built.

Framework

What Is a Long-term Value System?

A long-term value system is not simply about "long-termism." It is about how a system continues to create value, sustain coordination, transmit itself, and keep growing through change over time.

01
Creation
创造
How does an enterprise form genuine brand equity? How does an organization build distinctive core capabilities? How does a platform sustain the willingness of others to participate? Long-term value must first be created — not merely exchanged.
02
Coordination
协同
Value is never generated at a single point. It depends on teams, institutions, incentives, processes, trust, and the allocation of responsibility. Without coordination, even the best strategy dissipates in execution.
03
Transmission
传承
Mature value should not depend on any single key individual. It must be sedimented, transferred, institutionalized — and sustained across generations. Corporate governance, foundation governance, family succession, and academic training all ultimately address this question.
04
Restructuring
重构
Today's world is continuously disrupted by AI, technology, organizational change, and generational shift. A system that cannot restructure itself is unlikely to endure. Long-term value is not rigidly preserving the past — it is continuously renewing one's capacity boundaries through change.
A long-term value system is not simply about "long-termism"; it is about how a system continues to create value, sustain coordination, transmit itself, and keep growing through change over time.

框架

什么是长期价值系统？

长期价值系统讨论的，不只是"长期主义"，而是一个系统如何在时间中持续创造价值、维持协作、完成传承，并在变化中继续生长。

01
创造
Creation
一个企业如何形成真正的品牌资产？一个组织如何建立区别于他人的核心能力？一个平台如何让外界持续愿意参与？长期价值首先必须能够被创造，而不只是短期交换。
02
协同
Coordination
价值从来不是单点产生的。它依赖团队、制度、激励、流程、信任与责任分配来维持。没有协同，再好的战略都会在执行中流失。
03
传承
Transmission
真正成熟的价值，不应只依附于某一个关键人物。它必须能够被沉淀、被传递、被制度化，甚至跨代际延续。企业治理、基金会治理、家族传承与学术训练，本质上都与这一问题有关。
04
重构
Restructuring
今天的世界正被 AI、技术、组织变革与代际变化持续冲击。如果一套系统不能重构，就难以长期存在。长期价值不是僵化地守住过去，而是在变化中不断更新自己的能力边界。
长期价值系统讨论的，不只是"长期主义"，而是一个系统如何在时间中持续创造价值、维持协作、完成传承，并在变化中继续生长。

For Entrepreneurs

Why Entrepreneurs Need to Think in Systems

The real challenge for entrepreneurs is not making growth happen once. It is making growth that does not depend solely on luck, timing, traffic, or individual will.

Many enterprises grow fast in their early years through opportunity, instinct, and localized advantage. But over a longer arc, what determines whether they can continue is no longer just market opportunity — it is whether they have built the capacity to continuously create value, the organizational mechanisms to sustain coordination, the institutional design to support trust, the brand and culture that can transmit across cycles, and the ability to restructure their own capability system in the face of technological change.

What entrepreneurs truly need to build is not just enterprise scale. It is the enterprise's long-term value system.

对企业家而言

为什么企业家需要理解长期价值系统

对于企业家而言，真正的挑战并不是做出一次增长，而是如何让增长不只依赖运气、红利、流量或个人意志。

很多企业早期靠机会、直觉、拼劲和局部优势快速发展，但走到更长周期后，真正决定其能否继续走下去的，不再只是市场机会，而是是否拥有持续创造价值的能力、稳定协同的组织机制、支撑信任的制度设计、能够跨周期传递的品牌与文化，以及在技术变化面前持续重构自身能力系统的能力。

企业家真正要建立的，不只是企业规模，而是企业的长期价值系统。
